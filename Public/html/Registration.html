About Us

The Revolution is a soon to be non-governmental organization focused on youth empowerment. It hosts an annual event in Port Harcourt, Rivers State, Nigeria, and always tries to encourage the youth to not follow the norm and to stand out. The first Revolution program took place in 2015, and was believed to a success through the testimonies of the attendees. It was a fun-filled event that had a lot of entertainment showcasing local talents and a key speaker that motivated the youth to be the change they want to see in their surroundings and to make a difference. As a result oriented organization, our motto says everything we want the youth to do; Stand up! Be the change and make a difference.
